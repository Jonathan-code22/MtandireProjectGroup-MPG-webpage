About North Project Group (NPG)

North Project group(NPG) is a group of students pursuing Bachelor's degree in Human Science and Community Services at Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources. These are passionate students who conducted a reaserch to find more on THE EFFECTS OF TEENAGE PREGNANCY ON SOCIAL LIFE OF TEEN AGE MOTHERS. A CASE STUDY OF T/A NJEWA, MTANDIRE.

After a clear evaluation of the results, they went further to implement the interventions that they suggested. Therefore, pictures and videos displayed on this website reveal all activities during the project implementation.
